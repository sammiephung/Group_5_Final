Full Description

The matte black glass soy candle is an elegant and bold choice, perfect for groomsmen, black tie events, wedding candles, all-natural brands and small batch candle collections. The matte black vessel coupled with a custom label creates a dynamic yet minimalist contrast that grabs attention without overwhelming the space.

These hand poured custom scented candles are complete with gift-ready packaging and white-tip wood matches. Our candles are made with wax from 100% pure soybeans, farmed without pesticides, GMO-free, phthalate-free, and zero petroleum products or additives.
